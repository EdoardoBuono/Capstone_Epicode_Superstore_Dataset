This project is a **Business Intelligence dashboard developed in Power BI** to analyze sales performance using the Superstore dataset.

The goal of this project is to transform raw data into meaningful insights, supporting business understanding across multiple dimensions such as products, customers, geography and shipping.

---

## 📁 Dataset

The dataset used is the **Superstore dataset**, which includes information about:

- Orders  
- Products  
- Customers  
- Shipping modes  
- Sales, Profit and Discounts  

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

The original dataset consisted of a single large table.

To improve the data model and follow a structured BI approach, the dataset was cleaned and split in **Excel** into four main tables:

- **Sales** → order details, sales, profit, quantity and discounts  
- **Products** → product information, category and sub-category  
- **Customers** → customer data and segment  
- **Shipping** → shipping mode information  

These tables were then imported into Power BI and connected through relationships to build a **relational data model**.

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is divided into multiple pages, each focusing on a specific business area.

### 🏠 Home Page  
Navigation page that allows users to easily access all sections of the dashboard.

---

### 📊 Overview  
Provides a high-level summary of performance:

- Total Sales, Profit and Orders  
- Profit Margin  
- Sales and Profit Growth vs Last Year  
- Monthly trend comparison (Current vs Previous Year)  

---

### 💻 Product Analysis  
Focuses on product performance:

- Sales, Profit and Profit Margin by Category  
- Identification of the most profitable products  
- Top 10 products by profit  

---

### 🌎 Geographic Performance  
Analyzes performance across regions and states:

- Sales, Profit and Profit Margin by Region  
- Sales distribution map  
- Top performing states  

---

### 👥 Customer & Segment Analysis  
Explores customer behavior:

- Sales and Profit by Segment  
- Profit Margin comparison  
- Top customers by sales  
- Discount distribution by segment  

---

### 🚚 Sales & Shipping Insights  
Analyzes shipping performance and product demand:

- Orders, Sales and Profit by Shipping Mode  
- Units sold by product category  

---

### 📌 Key Insights  
Final page summarizing the main business findings derived from the analysis.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Sales decreased from 2014 to 2015, but profit increased, indicating improved profitability  
- Technology is the most profitable category, while Furniture shows much lower margins  
- The West region leads in sales and profit, but the East region has the highest profit margin  
- The Consumer segment drives most sales but has lower margins due to higher discount usage  
- Standard Class is the dominant shipping mode  
- Office Supplies has the highest number of units sold
